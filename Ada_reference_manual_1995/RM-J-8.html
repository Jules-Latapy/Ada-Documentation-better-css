J.8 Mod Clauses

Syntax

1
mod_clause ::= at mod static_expression;

Static Semantics

2
   A record_representation_clause of the form:
3
for r use
    record at mod a
        ...
    end record;
4
   is equivalent to:
5
for r'Alignment use a;
for r use
    record
        ...
    end record;
