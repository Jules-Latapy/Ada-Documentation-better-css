2   Lexical Elements

1/3
{AI05-0299-1} [The text of a program consists of the texts of one or more compilations. The text of a compilation is a sequence of lexical elements, each composed of characters; the rules of composition are given in this clause. Pragmas, which provide certain information for the compiler, are also described in this clause.]
