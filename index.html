Dont do

You should realy not critisize the king, and you should not begin a conversation about the royality, thai respect a lot the monarchy and it's forbiden to jock about the king.
For Thai, head is the highest part of the body, you should not touch someone head even in celebration.
Because feet are the lowest part, you should not point with feet.
Thai think about upper and lower, for exemple, you should not put trash on the table.
If you're a woman, don't try to touch monk, it's forbiden for the monk to touch woman. It's a bit impolite to point at something with something with finger, use youre all palm. Dont throw toilet paper inside the toilet, the sewer system is not made for it. When you enter temple and someone's home, you should take of your shoes.
