To do

Greeting

When you meet people, salute politely, for foreigner is not mandatory to waii at everybody.
The waii is a way of greeting and thanks in thailand,
to waii you should:
put you palms together, the up of your index should be at you shin. and you should bow you body slightly.
the high of you index is the amount of respect.
  1. for normal : shin
  2. for elder : nose
  3. for monk : eyebrow
  4. for royality : hairline

Politness

Smile if you want to begin a conversation.
Use polites particles at the end of your thai sentence, it always looks nice.

Miscelianous

When order a drink, ask for not too much ice cube, because they tend to put half the drink of ice.
Watch out when you retrieve the money, because sometime they will ask you if want to "make conversion", most of the time, your bank can handle the transaction for low cost, so say no.

Dont do

  1. You should really not criticize the king, and you should not begin a conversation about the reality, thai respect a lot the monarchy and it's forbidden to jock about the king.
  2. For Thai, head is the highest part of the body, you should not touch someone head even in celebration.
    Because foot are the lowest part, you should not point with your foot.
    Thai think about upper and lower, for exemple, you should not put trash on the table.
  3. If you're a woman, don't try to touch monk, it's forbiden for the monk to touch woman.
  4. It's a bit impolite to point at something with something with finger, use your full palm.
  5. Dont throw toilet paper inside the toilet, the sewer system is not made for it.
  6. When you enter temple or someone's home, you should take of your shoes.

To visit

Temples

In Thai culture, religion is very important and like churches in Europe, temples are everywhere. There is multiple kind of temple:
  1. Thai style temple
    • Wat arun, the temple of the dawn, is a old-style buddist temple, its in front of the river and is realy beautiful by the night.
    • Wat po, also known as the Temple of the Reclining Buddha, is a big temple.
    • Wat Rong Khun, the white temple is beautiful modern temple in the north of thailand, it's feel like it just came from heaven.
  2. Chinese style temple
    There is many kind of chinese temple, For instance the Wihan Phra Photisat Kuan Im, Is a Buddhist temple by the river and it got a lot of bright colors.
  3. Indian
    Indians temples are complex temples that features a lot of details and various colors.

Khǒn dance

Khǒn dance is a kind of drama, The actors wears beautiful and burlesque mask, there is an orchestra with a narrator (because actors cannot speak with mask),
It's a switch between gallants and martials dances.
